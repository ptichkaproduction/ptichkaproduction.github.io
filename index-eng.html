VideoSee - development history

  1. Temporary shutdown of video hosting for an indefinite period

    2025, January 6

    In 2024, the main server was damaged, causing the entire video hosting service to crash. Attempts were made to restore it, but they were to no avail. As a result, the video hosting service temporarily suspended its operations for an indefinite period.

  2. The greatest update

    2024, December 12

    During the long work, the system of accounts and related functions was implemented, therefore anyone can upload their video to this platform; the video hosting has entered beta testing status

  3. Interface redesign and improvement

    2024, October 10

    The project founder had problems with creating a YouTube analogue - the project looked like the very first site from the 90s. A developer friend (or Ptichka Prod. CEO) came to the rescue, redesigned and slightly improved the site

  4. Foundation

    2024, September 3

    The video hosting was created after YouTube slowed down in Russia (I HATE ROSKOMNADZOR!). The main idea is to create a decent YouTube replacement
